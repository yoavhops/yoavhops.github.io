Yes Mccan

Yes Mccan

Biography

As a member of Dead Obies, Yes Mccan has released two albums, one EP and two mixtapes, and performed over 150 concerts across the French-speaking world. He is set to release his debut solo album - the follow-up to the PS. Merci pour le love EP - in 2018.

Yes Mccan made his name on Montreal’s WordUP! rap battle circuit in the early 00s. It was then that he met some of the future members of Dead Obies with whom he has since released two albums, one EP and two mixtapes, and performed over 150 concerts across the French-speaking world.

On top of continuing his activities with Dead Obies, Yes Mccan has a leading role on the TV show Fugueuse (TVA) and is currently hard at work on his debut album, to be released in 2018.
